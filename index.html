Creating fun game experiences

Explore the world of Korsak in this turn-based strategy game inspired by classical tabletop games. Create and manage your unit groups, deploy them on the board and command them with strategic actions. There is no recruitment or resources management. Your units is all what you have. You must play your actions strategically to minimize casualties.

The game features single player campaign and multiplayer skirmish against AI-controlled players and online modes.
